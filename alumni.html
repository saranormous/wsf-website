WSF Alumni

Woodside Elementary alumni — your connection to your school community continues

Staying Connected

Once a Woodside Wildcat, always a Woodside Wildcat. Whether you graduated recently or decades ago, your time at Woodside Elementary shaped who you are — and the community you were part of is still thriving today.

Woodside School Foundation welcomes alumni who want to stay connected, give back, or simply keep up with news from the school. There are several easy ways to stay engaged with the community that shaped your early years.

We also invite alumni who are interested in giving back financially to consider supporting the Foundation. Your gift helps current students enjoy the same outstanding academic, cultural, and enrichment programs that made Woodside special for you. Every contribution — of any size — makes a direct difference in the lives of Woodside students today.

To be added to the alumni mailing list, or for any questions about reconnecting with the Woodside community, reach out to us at wsf@woodsideschool.us.

Ways to Stay Engaged

Join the Mailing List

Stay informed about upcoming events, school traditions, and Foundation news. We keep alumni updated on what's happening at Woodside so you never feel too far from campus.

Support the Foundation

Your gift to WSF helps current students enjoy the same exceptional education you received. WSF funds art, music, PE, science, language programs, and much more — programs that rely entirely on community generosity.

No matter the amount, your support makes a real difference.
