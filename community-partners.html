Community Partners

Good Schools are Everybody's Business!

Become a 2025–26 Community Partner

WSF raises approximately 20% of Woodside Elementary School's annual operating budget through its fundraising programs. Community Partners play a vital role in making that possible. Sponsorships begin at $1,000 and provide year-round visibility on campus, in the Grand Auction catalog, and across WSF communications.

To learn more or enroll, contact us at wsf@woodsideschool.us.

Frequently Asked Questions

Any local merchant or service provider in the greater Woodside area is welcome to become a Community Partner. Sponsorships are open to businesses of all sizes — from independent shops to established firms. We especially welcome businesses that serve Woodside families and share our commitment to supporting public education.

Partners receive a range of benefits depending on their sponsorship tier, including:

  • Year-round campus signage with direct visibility to Woodside families
  • Grand Auction catalog advertising, distributed to 350+ households
  • Promotion at the Grand Auction Gala, attended by 300+ community members
  • Annual report recognition
  • Website listing with a link to your business

WSF raises approximately 20% of Woodside Elementary School's annual operating budget. Without private fundraising, the school would be forced to cut programs like music, art, technology, and language instruction — and class sizes would increase. Community Partner sponsorships go directly toward sustaining these programs for every student at the school.

To become a Community Partner, email us at wsf@woodsideschool.us or ask the WSF office for an enrollment form. A member of the Community Partners committee will follow up with next steps and enrollment paperwork.
