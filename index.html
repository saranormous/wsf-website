Our Mission

Bridging the Gap Between Public Funding and Educational Excellence

The Woodside School Foundation exists to raise money that supplements the budget at Woodside Elementary School — ensuring continued excellence in academic, social, and cultural programs that make this community's school truly special.

The Numbers

Here's how your donations add up

Woodside School costs about $14 million a year to run — roughly $37,000 per student. Public funding covers $12 million. WSF fills the gap.

How We Raise Funds

Three Ways We Sustain Excellence

Annual Fund

Our primary fundraiser, running each fall. Families are asked to contribute $5,500 per student — the gap between state funding and the true cost of a Woodside education. Every gift matters, regardless of size.

Learn more

Grand Auction

Our beloved annual gala — running since 1984! Each spring, 300+ guests gather for cocktails, dinner, and a live auction. The 2026 theme is "Woodside Island." Tickets and sponsorships are available each spring.

Learn more

Community Partners

Local businesses sponsor WSF year-round in exchange for campus signage, catalog advertising, and community visibility. As we say: "Good Schools are Everybody's Business!" Partners range from $1,000 to $25,000.

Learn more
