About the Grand Auction

The WSF Grand Auction is one of the most anticipated events on the Woodside school calendar. Each spring, more than 300 guests gather for an evening of cocktails, a sit-down dinner, and a spirited live auction featuring extraordinary experiences, travel packages, art, and more.

The event has been a cornerstone of WSF fundraising since 1984 — over four decades of community celebration. Proceeds go directly toward the programs that make Woodside Elementary exceptional: music, art, language, technology, and more.

The Auction Catalog

Every year, WSF produces a full printed auction catalog distributed to more than 350 households in the Woodside community. The catalog features every item available in both the online silent auction and the live gala auction, giving bidders the chance to plan their bids in advance.

The online auction opens approximately one week before the gala, allowing community members who cannot attend in person to participate in the bidding.

Count-Me-In (CMI) Parties

One of the most beloved features of the Grand Auction is the Count-Me-In (CMI) party format. CMI parties are hosted by Woodside families and staff — intimate gatherings ranging from wine tastings and cooking nights to outdoor adventures. Anyone can join by bidding a fixed "count-me-in" price, making them a fun and accessible way to get involved even without attending the gala.

Student Art Contest

Each year, a student art contest is held in connection with the Grand Auction. Student artwork is featured in the auction catalog and displayed at the gala. The winning piece is used as the signature artwork for the event — a cherished tradition that puts students front and center in the celebration.
